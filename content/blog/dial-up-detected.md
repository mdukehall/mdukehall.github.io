## Why it looks like this

I graduated comp sci in **1998**. That means I came up on IRC, green-on-black terminals, and the particular joy of a well-tuned monospace font. This design is intentional nostalgia, not accidental ugliness.

## Writing posts

Each post needs frontmatter at the top:

```md
---
title: "Your title"
date: "2026-07-16"
excerpt: "One-line summary for the feed."
---

Your content here...
```

That's it. No CMS, no database — just files in the repo. Very old school. Very good.
